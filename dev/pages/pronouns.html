My Preferred Pronouns

Main Preference

I most prefer just my name instead of a pronoun.

Example: Suraj went to open Suraj's book. The book has been Suraj's since last year and kept close to Suraj.

Other Acceptable Pronouns

he/him/his

Example: He went to open his book. The book has been his since last year and kept close to him.

they/them/their/theirs

Example: They went to open their book. The book has been theirs since last year and kept close to them.
